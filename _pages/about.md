I am a first-year PhD student at the University of Waterloo, supervised by [Yaoliang Yu](https://cs.uwaterloo.ca/~y328yu/). My co-supervisor is [Iain Murray](https://homepages.inf.ed.ac.uk/imurray2/). Before starting my PhD, I obtained a Master of Mathematics from the University of Waterloo and a Bachelor of Science from the Technical University of Munich. My research interests are broadly in the area of machine learning, probabilistic modeling, and inference.
